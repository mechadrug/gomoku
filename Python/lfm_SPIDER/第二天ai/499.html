Introduction

Gaoling School of Artificial Intelligence (GSAI) is an official school affiliated to Renmin University of China (RUC). It is aiming to become a top school that can influence and shape the era of AI. Besides advancing AI theory and technology, GSAI is dedicated to bringing together AI and humanities, and exploring an “Intelligent and Warm” future for the world.
